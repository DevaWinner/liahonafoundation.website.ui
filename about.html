About Us

Our Mission

Who We Are

The Liahona Foundation is dedicated to empowering students in Nigeria by providing essential resources such as laptops and tuition assistance.

Our mission is to bridge the digital divide and ensure that quality education is accessible to all, regardless of socioeconomic status.

We believe that every student deserves the opportunity to succeed, and technology plays a crucial role in modern education.

Values

Our Values

Empowerment

We believe in empowering students with the tools and resources they need to succeed. By providing laptops and tuition assistance, we enable students to access quality education and bridge the digital divide. Our goal is to foster self-sufficiency and confidence in each student, helping them to realize their full potential.

Accessibility

We are committed to making education accessible to all. Regardless of socioeconomic background, every student deserves the opportunity to learn and grow. We strive to remove financial barriers and provide equitable access to educational resources, ensuring that all students have a chance to succeed.

Collaboration

We value the power of collaboration and partnership. By working with other like-minded organizations, we amplify our impact and reach. Together, we create a supportive network that enhances educational opportunities for students in Nigeria and beyond.

Activities

Our Approach

What We Do

  • By equipping students with laptops, we enable them to engage with digital learning platforms, access a wealth of information online, and develop the skills necessary for the digital age.
  • In addition to providing technology, we offer tuition assistance to alleviate the financial burdens that many families face. We strive to remove barriers to education, ensuring that talented and motivated students can pursue their academic goals without the worry of financial constraints.
  • At the Liahona Foundation, we are committed to creating a brighter future for the youth of Nigeria. By investing in education, we are not only transforming individual lives but also contributing to the broader development of communities and the nation as a whole. Our programs are designed to foster a love for learning, encourage innovation, and inspire the next generation of leaders.
